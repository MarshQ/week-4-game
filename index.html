Rupee Scramble

Welcome to the hyrule rupee game!
Ganon has altered all of the values of the rupees in hyrule! It is up to you to undo his mischief!
In order to do this you must collect rupees to find out their values (do this by clicking on the rupees), and gather the required amount of rupees to reverse the magic (required number is the green number)
Be warned though, ganon has released his minions to steal away your rupees if you gather too many! Thus if you happen to gather even one more than the required amount they will steal them away from you and ganon will shuffle the values of each rupee up once again!
Good Luck Hero go and save the day!
